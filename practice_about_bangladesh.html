About Bangladesh

Bangladesh is a dynamic, densely populated South Asian country located in the fertile delta of the Padma and Jamuna rivers. It shares land borders with India and Myanmar, and opens to the Bay of Bengal. Famous for its resilient people and natural beauty, the nation has evolved into a global manufacturing hub.

Economic Growth

As the second-largest exporter of readymade garments globally, Bangladesh has maintained one of the strongest and fastest-growing economies in South Asia.

Breathtaking Nature

It is home to both the largest continuous natural sea beach (Cox's Bazar) and the largest mangrove forest on Earth (the Sundarbans).

Rich Culture

Steeped in history, art, and vibrant storytelling, the nation’s culture is a unique fusion shaped by centuries of Muslim, Buddhist and Hindu influences.
