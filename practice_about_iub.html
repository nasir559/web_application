About IUB

Independent University, Bangladesh (IUB) is a leading private research university located in the Bashundhara Residential Area of Dhaka. Established in 1993, it offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs across multiple schools, focusing on research, global partnerships, and holistic student development.

You can also visit the official IUB website.
